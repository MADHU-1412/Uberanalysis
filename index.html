1. Overview Analysis

Vehicle Type: UberX dominated trip volume and revenue, while premium rides (Uber Black, Uber Comfort) contributed fewer rides but higher average fares.

Payments: Majority of trips were paid via Uber Pay, highlighting a strong preference for cashless transactions.

Locations: Frequent pickup/drop-off points included Penn Station, Madison Square West, and Upper East Side.

KPIs:

  • Total Bookings: 103.7K
  • Total Booking Value: $1.6M
  • Avg Booking Value: $15
  • Total Distance: 349K miles
  • Avg Trip Time: 16 minutes

2. Time Analysis

Peak Hours: Bookings consistently rose from 8 AM ‚Äì 8 PM, with the midday period showing the highest demand.

Weekly Trend:

  • Saturday & Sunday had the highest trips (weekend peak).
  • Friday dipped slightly compared to weekends, likely due to late-night rides shifting into Saturday.

Heatmap Patterns: Clear morning (8‚Äì10 AM) and evening (5‚Äì7 PM) spikes, reflecting commuter behavior.

3. Trip Details

The dataset allowed drill-down into individual trip metrics (Trip ID, Vehicle Type, Fare, Distance, Pickup & Drop-off).

Example: A trip from LaGuardia Airport ‚Üí Midtown cost around $27 for ~9 miles (UberX).

This trip-level visibility supports deep-dive analysis into customer behavior, route preferences, and pricing fairness.
